## <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">3회차 헌법 빈출 part. 헌법의 개정 , 대한민국 헌정사</span>

### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold"><span style="color: blue">제7차 개정헌법</span></span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">유신헌법</span></span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">1972년</span> 제<span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">4</span>공화국 헌법</span>

### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">ⓛ 배경</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: black">1972년 10월 전국</span> <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">비상계엄</span> 선포, <span style="color: black">비상조치 결행</span></span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: black">국회 권한을 비상국무회의가 넘겨받아 헌법개정안 의결 → 국민투표로 확정</span></span>

### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">② 정부형태</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: black">대통령(</span>임기 <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">6년</span>)에게 <span style="color: black">강력한 권한 부여</span></span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: black">긴급조치권</span>, 국회의원 정수 <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">3분의 1</span> 추천권 등</span>

### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">③ 통일주체국민회의</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">통일주체국민회</span><span style="color: black; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">의 신설</span></span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: black">대통령 직선제 폐지</span> → 토론 없이 <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">무기명투표</span>로 <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">간선제</span> 선출</span>

<span style="color: black">(박정희 대통령: <span style="color: black">비상국무회의</span>에서 선출)</span>

<span style="color: black">국회의원 중 정수 <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">3분의 1</span> 에 해당하는 국회의원 선거 (23 경찰간부)</span>

### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">④ 기본권</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">본질적 내용침해금지조항 삭제</span></span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">체포·구속적부심사제도 폐지</span></span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">언론·출판의 검열금지조항 삭제</span></span>

### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">⑤ 헌법개정 이원화</span>

<span style="color: black">대통령 제안 개헌안: <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">국민투표</span>로 확정</span>

<span style="color: black">국회의원 제안 개헌안: <span style="color: purple; font-weight: bold">국회 의결</span> → <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">통일주체국민회의 의결</span>로 확정 (절차 <span style="color: purple; font-weight: bold">이원화</span>)</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: blue; font-weight: bold">제8차 개정헌법</span> (<span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">1980년</span>) / <span style="color: blue; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">제5공화국 헌법</span></span>

<span style="color: black">**① 배경**</span>

<span style="color: black">**10.26.으로 인해 박정희 대통령 작고 후 전두환 중심으로 정권 확립**</span>

<span style="color: black">**② 정부형태**</span>

<span style="color: black">**대통령의 임기를 **<span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">7년</span> **단임으로 규정하고,** <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">대통령선거인단</span>**에 의한** <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">무기명투표</span>**로 선거**</span>

<span style="color: black">**긴급조치권을 폐지한 대신 국회해산권 부여**</span>

<span style="color: black">**③ 기본권**</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">행복추구권</span>, <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">사생활의 비밀과 자유</span>, <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">무죄추정의 원칙</span>, <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">연좌제금지원칙</span>, <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">환경권</span>, <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">소비자보호</span>, <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">적정임금조항 신설</span> (기출다수)</span>

<span style="color: black">**④ 경제질서**</span>

<span style="color: black">**자유시장경제질서를 기본으로 하면서도 사회적 기본권을 일부 도입하는 등 사회적 시장경제질서를 지향**</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: blue; font-weight: bold">제9차 개정헌법</span> (<span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">1987년</span>) / <span style="color: blue; font-weight: bold">현행헌법</span></span>

<span style="color: black">**① 배경**</span>

<span style="color: black">**1987년 6월항쟁 이후 헌정사상 최초로 여야 합의를 거친 헌법개정안이** <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">국회의 의결</span>**을 거쳐**</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">국민투표</span>**로 확정**</span>

<span style="color: black">**② 정부형태**</span>

<span style="color: black">**대통령을** <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">직선제</span>**로 선출, 임기는** <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">5년</span> **단임**</span>

<span style="color: black">**③ 국회**</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">국정감사권</span>**이 부활되어** <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">국정조사권</span>**과 국정감사권이 동시에 인정**</span>

<span style="color: black">**④** <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">헌법재판소</span>**가 부활되어 위헌법률심판권과 탄핵심판을 담당.**</span>

<span style="color: black">**⑤ 기본권**</span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">적법절차조항</span>, 형사피해자의 <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">재판절차진술권</span>, <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">범죄피해자구조청구권</span>, <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">최저임금조항 신설</span></span>

<span style="color: black"><span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">언론·출판의 검열금지조항</span> <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">부활</span> (기출다수)</span>
