### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">✅  <span style="color: blue; font-weight: bold">인과관계</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">**어떤 행위라도 죄의 요소되는** <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold">위험발생</span>**에 연결되지 아니한 때에는** <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">그 결과</span>**로 인하여 벌하지 아니한다.**

</span>

<span style="color: black">👉  **결과가 행위랑 관련 없으면 처벌 안 함**


</span>

### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">✅  <span style="color: blue; font-weight: bold">사실의 착오</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">**특별히** <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold">무거운 죄</span>**가 되는 사실을** <span style="color: purple; font-weight: bold">인식하지 못한 행위</span>**는** <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">무거운 죄로 벌하지 아니한다.</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">👉  **중한 죄라는 걸 몰랐다면, 그 중한 죄로는 못 벌함**


</span>

### <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">✅  <span style="color: blue; font-weight: bold">구성요건 착오 - 학설 비교 핵심</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">🔹  <span style="color: purple; font-weight: bold">구체적 객체의 착오</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">**예: A인 줄 알고 B를 살해**

</span>

<span style="color: black">**전 학설 공통 →** <span style="color: black; font-weight: bold">고의에 의한 살인죄</span> **성립**

</span>

<span style="color: black">🔹  <span style="color: purple; font-weight: bold">구체적 방법의 착오</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">**예: A 쏘려다** <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold">빗나가서</span> **B가 죽음**

</span>

<span style="color: black">**구체적 부합설:** A에 대한 <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">살인미수</span> **+** B에 대한 <span style="color: black; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">과실치사</span> **(상상적 경합)**

</span>

<span style="color: black">**법정적 부합설(판례):** B에 대한 <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold">살인죄</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">**추상적 부합설:** B에 대한 <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold">살인죄</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">✅  <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">시험 핵심</span> **→** <span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">구체적 부합설만 결론 다름</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">**예①: 개인 줄 알고 쐈는데 사람이 맞음**

</span>

<span style="color: black">**구체적/법정적 부합설:** <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">손괴미수</span> **+** <span style="color: black; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">과실치사</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">**추상적 부합설:** <span style="color: black; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">손괴기수</span> **+** <span style="color: black; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">과실치사</span>

</span>

<span style="color: black">**예②: 사람인 줄 알고 쐈는데 개가 맞음**

</span>

<span style="color: black">**구체적/법정적 부합설:** <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold">살인미수만</span> **성립 (**<span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">과실손괴는 불가벌</span>**)**

</span>

<span style="color: black">**추상적 부합설:** <span style="color: black; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">손괴기수</span> **+** <span style="color: purple; text-decoration: underline; font-weight: bold">살인미수</span>
